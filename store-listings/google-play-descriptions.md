## Full description (under 4000 characters)

```
Can’t decide what to eat? Meal of Fortune helps you pick meals in seconds—with mini-games, a spin wheel, and group voting.

► HOW IT WORKS

• Save meals you like to “Meals I want” and spin the wheel when you’re stuck. One tap and you’ve got dinner (or breakfast or lunch).

• Play the mini-game: swipe and tap through bases, proteins, and cooking methods. Get a random combo (e.g. “Grilled chicken rice with garlic”) and add it to your calendar.

• Plan your week: add chosen meals to your calendar, set reminders, and see what’s coming up.

• Decide with others: create groups (e.g. roommates, family), start a vote (“What’s for dinner?”), and let everyone suggest and vote on meals. No more endless “I don’t care—you pick.”

► FEATURES

• Spin wheel – Pick from your saved meals when you can’t choose.
• Mini-game – Three quick rounds (base, protein/veg, method) for a random meal combo.
• Calendar – Plan meals by day, set reminders, and keep track of what you’re eating.
• Groups & voting – Create groups, invite by code, run time-limited votes, and see results in real time.
• Friends – Add friends by code, organize with categories, and see who’s in your groups.
• Profile & privacy – Set your display name, optional bio and photo, and control who sees your friend code and preferences.

► WHO IT’S FOR

• Anyone who stares at the fridge or menu and thinks “I don’t know.”
• Households and roommates who want to decide together without the back-and-forth.
• People who like a bit of fun (spin, mini-game) instead of a boring list.

No ads. Your data stays yours—we don’t sell it. Add your meals, spin or play, plan your calendar, and decide with friends. That’s Meal of Fortune.
```
